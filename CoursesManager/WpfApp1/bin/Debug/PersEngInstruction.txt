Add claim - allows you to add your claim, for this you need to fill in the following fields:
  Name - your name 
  Surname - your last Name
  Format - class format
  Language - language
  Level - language preparation level
  Intensity - intensity of classes per week
Auto-complete fields:
  Duration - the duration of the course (depending on the intensity)
  Price - price (depending on the format and intensity)
  ID - your personal number
Change count of money - count of your money
Return - return to guest window
